## FAQ Section

**Q: Is this really free?**
A: Yes. The software is 100% free and open source. Optional paid tiers ($3-8/mo) add cloud backup and extra features, but the core is free forever.

**Q: Do I need to be technical?**
A: If you can install an app, you can do this. We have a one-command Docker installer. 95% of setup is automatic.

**Q: What cameras work?**
A: Anything. USB webcams, IP cameras, RTSP streams, even your old laptop camera. If it produces video, it works.

**Q: Can police access my cameras?**
A: Absolutely not. Your cameras, your server, your control. We never touch your video data.

**Q: What if I already use Frigate or MotionEye?**
A: Perfect! Just install our plugin. Keep using what you love, we just add the mobile app and public cam features.

**Q: What about bandwidth?**
A: Public cams use peer-to-peer WebRTC. Your video streams directly to viewers. We don't store or relay it (unless you want cloud backup).

## Notes on Tone & Messaging

**Primary Message:** "Take back control from Big Tech surveillance"

**Secondary Message:** "Community-powered public webcam directory"

**Brand Personality:**
- Anti-establishment but not paranoid
- Technical but accessible
- Community-focused, not corporate
- Privacy-obsessed but not preachy

**Keywords to Emphasize:**
- Privacy, control, open-source, community
- Self-hosted, local processing
- No subscriptions, no police access
- Your data, your hardware

**Avoid:**
- Sounding too paranoid or conspiracy-theory
- Being too technical (save that for docs)
- Comparing directly to competitors (let users draw conclusions)
- Over-promising on ease of use
