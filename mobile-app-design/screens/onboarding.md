# Onboarding Flow

**Layout:**

- A series of welcome screens that highlight the key features of the app.
- A screen that requests permissions for notifications and camera access.
- A guided flow to help users add their first camera.

**Components:**

- **Welcome Screens:** Illustrations and brief text explaining the benefits of the app (privacy, self-hosting, community).
- **Permissions Screen:** A clear explanation of why each permission is needed, with a button to grant the permissions.
- **Add Camera Flow:** Options for scanning a QR code or manual entry, with clear instructions for each step.

**Interactions:**

- **Swipe through welcome screens:** Users can swipe left and right to navigate through the welcome screens.
- **Tap "Grant Permissions":** Opens the system dialog to grant permissions.
- **Follow the guided flow:** The app will guide the user through the process of adding their first camera, with clear instructions and feedback at each step.
