Privacy Policy

Effective: [BETA] | Last Updated: November 4, 2025

üîí Our Privacy Promise

webcam.org is built on one principle: Your cameras, your data, your control.

Unlike Ring, Nest, and other corporate surveillance platforms, we believe privacy is a right, not a feature you pay for.

1. What We Collect

‚úÖ Data We DO Collect:

Account Information:

  • Email address (for login)
  • Username (public display name)
  • Password (encrypted with bcrypt - we cannot see it)
  • Subscription tier (free, plus, pro)

Camera Metadata (NOT Video):

  • Camera name, description, type
  • GPS location (only if you mark camera public)
  • Technical details (resolution, FPS)
  • Last seen timestamp
  • Thumbnail images (public cameras only)

Mobile App Data:

  • Device type (iOS/Android)
  • Push notification token
  • App version

Usage Analytics (Anonymized):

  • Pages visited (via Google Analytics)
  • Feature usage
  • Error logs (to fix bugs)

2. What We Do NOT Collect

‚ùå Data We DO NOT Collect:

  • Your private camera video feeds (video stays on your device)
  • Live surveillance footage (we cannot watch your cameras)
  • Audio recordings (unless you upload clips)
  • Biometric data (facial recognition, fingerprints)
  • Precise home address (only GPS if you share publicly)
  • Payment card details (handled by Stripe)
  • Passwords in plain text (encrypted)

3. Technical Proof

Private Cameras:

  • Stream via WebRTC peer-to-peer (direct device-to-device)
  • Or through Tailscale VPN (encrypted tunnel)
  • We only provide "signaling" (helping devices find each other)
  • We NEVER see your video

Self-Hosted Software:

  • All AI detection runs on YOUR hardware
  • Video stored on YOUR hard drive or cloud storage
  • Our plugin only sends event metadata (not video)

Open Source:

  • Our code is on GitHub (link coming soon)
  • Security researchers can verify these claims
  • No hidden data collection

4. How We Use Your Information

To Provide the Service:

  • Authenticate your login
  • Register and manage your cameras
  • Send push notifications (person detected, doorbell press)
  • Display public cameras on the map
  • Process payments (via Stripe)

What We Do NOT Do:

  • ‚ùå Sell your data to third parties
  • ‚ùå Show you targeted ads
  • ‚ùå Share data with police without legal process
  • ‚ùå Train AI models on your private footage
  • ‚ùå Use your cameras for our own surveillance

5. Data Sharing

What We Share Publicly (if you mark camera public):

  • Camera name, description, GPS location
  • Thumbnail image
  • Live stream (visible to anyone)

Service Providers We Use:

  • Firebase (push notifications)
  • Stripe (payments - PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Amazon S3 (cloud backup if enabled)
  • Google Analytics (anonymized stats)

Legal Requirements:

  • We may disclose data if required by valid subpoena or court order
  • We notify users before disclosure unless legally prohibited
  • We require proper legal process (no informal police requests)
  • Unlike Ring: We have NO agreements with police for warrantless access

6. Your Rights

You Can:

  • View all your account data in settings
  • Export your camera metadata (JSON format)
  • Download your cloud backup clips
  • Edit account information anytime
  • Make cameras public or private
  • Disable cloud backup
  • Delete your entire account (permanent)

GDPR (EU Users):

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification (correct errors)
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Contact: gdpr@webcam.org

CCPA (California Users):

  • Right to know what data is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of "sale" (we don't sell data)
  • Contact: privacy@webcam.org

7. Data Storage & Security

Where Data is Stored:

  • User data: PostgreSQL database (US East)
  • Metadata: Encrypted at rest
  • Video clips (if cloud backup): Amazon S3 encrypted
  • Passwords: Bcrypt hashed

Security Measures:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all connections
  • Two-factor authentication available
  • Regular security audits
  • Encrypted database backups

Data Retention:

  • Account data: Until you delete your account
  • Public camera listings: Until you remove from directory
  • Cloud backup clips: 7 days (Plus) or 30 days (Pro), then auto-deleted
  • Logs: 90 days for security

8. Children's Privacy

webcam.org is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we discover a child's data, we delete it immediately.

9. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be announced via email and in-app notification. Continued use after changes = acceptance.

10. Contact Us

  • Privacy Questions: privacy@webcam.org
  • GDPR (EU): gdpr@webcam.org
  • Security Issues: security@webcam.org
  • General Support: support@webcam.org

Our Commitment

Privacy is not a feature you pay for at webcam.org - it's our foundation.

  • We will never sell your data
  • We will never give warrantless police access
  • We will notify you before complying with legal requests (when legally allowed)
  • We will keep our code open source so you can verify these promises
