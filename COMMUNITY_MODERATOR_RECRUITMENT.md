# webcam.org - Community Moderator Recruitment Plan

**Author:** Gemini
**Status:** In Progress

This document outlines the plan for recruiting, training, and managing community moderators for the webcam.org platform.

## üöÄ Recruitment

### Phase 1: Initial Recruitment (Beta Phase)

- **Source:** We will recruit our first moderators from our most active and trusted community members on Discord and Reddit (r/selfhosted).
- **Criteria:**
  - Active and helpful in the community.
  - Demonstrated understanding of the project's mission and values.
  - No history of violating community guidelines.

### Phase 2: Open Recruitment (Post-Launch)

- **Application Form:** We will create a formal application form for users who are interested in becoming moderators.
- **Requirements:**
  - Must be a registered user for at least 3 months.
  - Must have a good standing in the community.
  - Must be at least 18 years old.

---

## üéì Training

All new moderators will go through a training program that covers:

- **Moderation Guidelines:** A thorough review of the `MODERATION_GUIDELINES.md` document.
- **Review Process:** A step-by-step guide on how to review new camera submissions and handle user reports.
- **Tools:** An introduction to the moderation tools, including the admin dashboard and communication channels.
- **Shadowing:** New moderators will shadow experienced moderators for a week before they are given full moderation privileges.

- **Admin Dashboard:** Moderators will have access to a special admin dashboard where they can review new cameras, handle reports, and manage users.
- **Private Discord Channel:** We will create a private Discord channel for moderators to communicate with each other and with the core team.
- **Regular Meetings:** We will hold regular online meetings with the moderation team to discuss challenges, review policies, and provide feedback.

- **Volunteer Position:** The community moderator role is a volunteer position.

- **Perks:** We may offer perks such as free access to paid features or exclusive merchandise.
