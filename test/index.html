Tips & Troubleshooting

Blurry or Dark Video?

  • Clean your camera lens
  • Improve lighting (consider a ring light)
  • Try selecting 1080p in camera settings
  • Check camera focus (if adjustable)

Camera Not Working?

  • Close other apps using the camera (Zoom, Teams, Skype)
  • Grant camera permissions in your browser
  • Try a different browser
  • Restart your browser or computer
  • Check device privacy settings

Upgrade Your Setup

  • Only 720p? Consider upgrading to a 1080p webcam
  • Recommended: Logitech C922 (1080p, 30fps)
  • Premium: Logitech Brio (4K, HDR)
  • Add a ring light for professional look

Your Privacy Matters

Your media stays on your device. All camera and microphone testing runs locally in your browser using standard web APIs.

No video, audio, or images are uploaded to our servers. The test page works offline once loaded.

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use this tool and improve the experience. No personally identifiable camera/audio data is collected.
