Download webcam.org Desktop

Turn your old laptop into a security camera

Download for Windows

Version 0.1.0 • Windows 10/11 • Free & Open Source

What's Included

  • Auto-detect any USB webcam (built-in or external)
  • AI-powered motion detection (runs locally)
  • Push notifications to your phone when motion detected
  • Optional: Share your camera publicly on webcam.org
  • Privacy-first: Your video stays on your computer
  • Runs in background (system tray)
  • Auto-start on Windows boot

System Requirements

  • Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit)
  • USB webcam or built-in laptop camera
  • Internet connection (for push notifications)
  • ~200 MB disk space

How It Works

  1. Install: Run the .exe installer (one-click setup)
  2. Select Camera: App auto-detects your USB webcam
  3. Done! Motion detection starts automatically
  4. Get Notified: Download the mobile app to receive push alerts
